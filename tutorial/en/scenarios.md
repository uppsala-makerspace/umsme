# Existing members
* Member creates an account using the same email that's in our records
* Member confirms their email (not required for SSO with Google)
* Member approves the latest version of the liability agreement
* Member can see their membership status, payments, etc.
* Member can open doors (if they have a lab membership)

# Existing member renews membership
* Member receives a reminder that the membership needs to be renewed soon (14 days before via email, more frequently in the app via push notifications)
* Member chooses a membership type in the app and initiates payment via Swish
* Payment happens in the Swish app and the member returns to the app
* Member receives a confirmation email for the completed payment
* Member can see the new membership status and the payment in the app
* Member can open doors (if they have a lab membership)

# New members
* Member creates an account in the app
* Member confirms their email (not required for SSO with Google)
* Member chooses a membership type in the app and initiates payment via Swish
* Payment happens in the Swish app and the member returns to the app
* Member receives a confirmation email for the completed payment
* Member can see the new membership status and the payment in the app
* A board member approves the new member and the system sends a welcome email
* Member approves the latest version of the liability agreement
* Member can open doors (if they have a lab membership)
